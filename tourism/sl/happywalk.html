1. ARTIST BOOTHS:
explore, buy, and commission works from various artists

2. COLLABORATIVE COLORING MURAL:
take a copy of any square from the mural, save the texture to your computer, color it in, then upload your colored texture (COPY/MOD) and share it with rainydew.

if you cannot upload textures, post your texture to a filesharing website (send.vis.ee, imgur, etc) and send the link to rainydew to upload it for you.

3. OPEN STAGE:
the stage is open to anyone who would like to share a special talent like DJing, performance, etc. you are also permitted to share music!
